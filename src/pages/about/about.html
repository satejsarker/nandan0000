Nandan TV

Nandan, a community-based infotainment Canadian Bengali Television, is a sister concern of Darkroom Media House Ltd. Its programs are broadcast from Toronto.

The channel wants to reach out to the Bengali community in North America as well as other parts of the world.

Its official transmission began on August, 2017. Run by a bunch of intelligent people, experienced in mass media production in Canada and abroad.

Nandan is a 24-hour worldwide HD streaming channel focused on broadcasting innovative programs on entertainment, information, education and so on.

Nandan can be watched online at Nandan.TV and through any IP boxes, Android, IOS, YouTube and Facebook too. Nandan is the voice of Bangladeshi Canadian community as well as of the Bengali-speaking people living in different parts of the world.

The channel works with local talents and encourages the community members to work with the team and do something substantial for the community with their great ideas.
